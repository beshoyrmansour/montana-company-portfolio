# Cookie Policy

We keep cookies to an absolute minimum. This page explains exactly what is stored on your device, why, and how to change your choice.

## Our approach

Montana does not use advertising, profiling, or cross-site tracking cookies. We never sell or share your data with advertisers.

## Cookies we use

| Name | Type | Purpose | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `cookie-consent` | Strictly necessary | Remembers your cookie choice (accept or decline) so we don't ask again. It is set only after you interact with the banner and stores your decision plus the date and policy version. | 180 days |

This is the only cookie we set. It is exempt from consent because it is strictly necessary to honour your own privacy choice.

## Analytics — only with your consent

If you click **Accept**, we load [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/data-policy) — a privacy-friendly, EU-hosted, cookieless analytics tool. Plausible:

- sets **no cookies** and uses no persistent identifiers;
- does **not** collect or store personal data or full IP addresses;
- produces only aggregate statistics (page views, referrers, country, device type).

If you click **Decline**, no analytics is loaded at all. Until you make a choice, nothing non-essential runs.

## Managing or withdrawing your choice

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time using the **Cookie settings** link in the website footer — withdrawing is as easy as giving consent. You can also delete cookies through your browser settings.

## More information

See our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy) for how we handle data you submit through forms and your rights under the EU GDPR and Egypt's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
